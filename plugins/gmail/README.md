Gmail helps you triage inbox activity, draft replies, and manage email threads faster.

## What It Can Do
- Summarizes inbox activity and surfaces high-priority threads.
- Drafts replies and follow-ups based on conversation context.
- Helps organize email workflows for faster response handling.

## Why Use It
- Cuts time spent triaging large inboxes.
- Improves response quality with context-aware drafts.
- Keeps email work from interrupting deep technical tasks.

## How It Works
1. Connect Gmail in Codex.
2. Ask for inbox triage, summaries, or reply drafting.
3. Review and send refined responses through your normal email process.

## Quick Examples
- `Summarize inbox, draft replies, or process email threads`

## Requirements
- A connected Gmail integration with mailbox access.
