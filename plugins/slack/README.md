Slack helps you summarize channels, draft responses, and keep team communication moving.

## What It Can Do
- Summarizes channel activity and drafts context-aware replies.
- Builds digests and triage views for notifications and team updates.
- Supports outbound message composition workflows for clear communication.

## Why Use It
- Reduces interrupt-driven context switching from high-volume channels.
- Improves consistency and quality of stakeholder communication.
- Helps teams stay aligned with less manual status effort.

## How It Works
1. Connect Slack in Codex.
2. Choose the workflow type (summary, triage, draft reply, outgoing update).
3. Review and send final messages through your Slack process.

## Quick Examples
- `Summarize unread activity, draft replies, or prepare updates`

## Requirements
- A connected Slack integration with access to relevant channels.
